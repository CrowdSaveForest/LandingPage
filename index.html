Crowdsave Forest

Open Source Initiative for Fighting Deforestation

The problem of deforestation and climate change is too serious to leave in the hands of slow moving corporations and politicians. Since we are the last generation that can make a worthy impact, we should take control of the issue and force through a solution.

We are a group of people dedicated to revolutionize the way to stop deforestation. Our backgrounds are from IT consultancy, Finance and Management Consulting, and we all hold master’s degrees in various fields. Most of us are in the mid 20s and we are located in Copenhagen and Oslo. The project was started out of pure frustration over the insufficient success that existing initiatives have had over the years.

The concept we propose has the potential to be swift, flexible, efficient, and free of corporate, individual, and political incentives. We want to establish this concept for free together with likeminded to create a powerful platform for change.

Join us and make it happen!

The Concept

Current organizations working against deforestation, and other causes, are too slow abd incur large administration fees.They are stuck in the last century. In the last decade we have witnessed the emergence of new business models brought through IT innovations.

We propose a platform that does something similar to the NGO industry. We want the platform to be open for everyone, free from any charges and control to be fully democratic and set by its users. That’s the vision.

We want to accomplish this by implementing:

  • 1
  • An environment where everyone can contribute on the same terms.
  • 2
  • Users upload prospects of forest and users control that these prospects are in fact for real. This requires high focus to avoid scams.
  • 3
  • Donators can choose to donate to specific prospects and not to a non-transparent fund. If we are not able to buy the forest, money will be returned.
  • 4
  • Donations never go through the organization but through a 3rd party such as major law firms, banks, revisioning firms to facilitate the transactions from user base to the seller of land. This will ensure all funds are far away from anyone in the organization, and will therefore make any kind of profit impossible.
  • 5
  • Protection of land will also be a part. This is something we are looking into.
  • 6
  • When land is bought we want to create a legal framework that makes it impossible to sell it in the future so that the organization can never be turned into a profit organization.
  • 7
  • We want the development and maintenance of the platform to be democratic with no one person or group having control. How do we ensure this?

Built and Maintained by Everyone

An open source platform for handling forest prospects.

Forest prospects can be uploaded to the platform by anyone.

Once verified, the prospects will be made available for donations.

Crowdfunding

Anyone can donate to specific prospect acquisitions or to preservation of already owned land.

Acquired donations are held safe by a trusted 3rd party such as a major bank, and transferred directly to each project. Since donations are never funneled through the organization, absolute non-profit is ensured.

Land Acquisition

The organization owns acquired prospects.

Land will be held indefinitely ensured by legal frameworks.

Land Preservation

Land is maintained and protected from deforestation.

Preservation strategies are specifically tailored to each land area.

However, what is lacking is a platform that can push through a real collaborative impact. We want to establish this for free together with likeminded to create a powerful platform for change.
